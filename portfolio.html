Featured Project: Big Rig Cookbook

Big Rig Cookbook is a health and wellness app for truck drivers. At its core, it will contain a repository of recipes written by truckers that can be prepared in a semi-truck. The app will also feature a fitness section where truckers can explore exercises that can be done on the road.

The mission is to improve the welfare of America's truckers, who are the lifeblood of the country.

I understand what it's like living in a truck, having done so myself. I hope to help truckers retake power over their health, and to show them that the world cares about them.
