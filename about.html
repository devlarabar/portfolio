About Me

Hi, I'm Lara! I'm a creative web developer based in Jacksonville, FL. I have a passion for creating responsive, effective web apps with intuitive interfaces to provide a seamless and enjoyable user experience on any device. I started coding incredibly young - I was eight when I first started learning HTML and CSS, and in high school when I picked up JavaScript.

I've worn many hats throughout the years: I was in the military, earned a bachelor's degree in psychology, and owned a business in the freight-logistics industry where I delivered freight across the country. These experiences have taught me the value of clear and kind communication, attention to detail, and patience, and offer me a unique problem-solving perspective in the world of software development.

I have a proven track record of working with end users to deliver high-quality products both through my freelance work and my contributions to online software. Working with the folks who will actually be using something I'm creating is an incredibly rewarding and humbling experience!

When I'm not coding, I enjoy making art, lifting things, playing with my cat Gizmo and gaming with my wonderful husband.
