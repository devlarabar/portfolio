Is your business accessible to everyone on the web?

In today's world, web presence is a must for any business - and not just on a computer, either. With more and more people accessing the web on mobile devices, responsive and accessible web pages are essential. I can create a responsive website for your business, or update your existing website to be accessible on all devices. All of my websites and web applications are responsive, intuitive, and effective:

Responsive

Over 60% of web traffic comes from mobile devices, so my websites work on all screen sizes.

Intuitive

Websites should be easy to use, with clear navigation and intuitive design.

Effective

Your message and brand will be presented clearly to your customers.

My Process

I take pride in creating and delivering projects that not only meet, but exceed your expectations. In order to provide the best service, and facilitate clear and consistent communication, our process of working together will look like so:

  1. 1

    ConsultationYou and I have a conversation where you share your goals, vision, and expectations for the project. In other words, we figure out what this project is about.

  2. 2

    ProposalI will provide you with a detailed proposal, outlining the plan of action for the project, as well as the timeline for completion.

  3. 3

    CreationI create and test the project, keeping you updated regularly on my progress while taking and implementing any feedback you may have.

  4. 4

    Delivery & LaunchI launch your final product onto your web server for the world to enjoy!
